Why Donate Blood?

A single blood donation can save multiple lives. Blood is required for accident victims, surgeries, cancer treatments, childbirth emergencies, and many other critical situations. Your donation can become someone's second chance at life.

How Our System Works

1. Users register and become blood donors.

2. Donor details are stored securely in the system.

3. Patients submit blood requests during emergencies.

4. The system searches matching donors based on blood group and city.

5. Patients can quickly contact suitable donors and receive help.
